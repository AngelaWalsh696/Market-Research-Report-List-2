<p><ul><li>Fixed Aerators</li><li>Floating Aerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerators market is categorized primarily into fixed and floating aerators. Fixed aerators are stationary devices, often installed in ponds or wastewater treatment facilities, designed to enhance oxygen transfer by utilizing static mechanisms or diffusers. In contrast, floating aerators are mobile systems that float on the water's surface, driven by motors or propellers, effectively agitating water to promote oxygen exchange. Each type serves unique applications in water treatment, aquaculture, and environmental management, ensuring optimal oxygen levels for aquatic ecosystems.</p></p>
