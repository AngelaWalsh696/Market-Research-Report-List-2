<p><p>The MRI-compatible pacemaker market consists of three main types: single-chamber pacemakers, which monitor and manage one heart chamber; dual-chamber pacemakers, designed to coordinate the activity of both the atrium and ventricle; and biventricular or cardiac resynchronization therapy (CRT) pacemakers, which stimulate all three heart chambers to improve heart efficiency. These devices enable patients with pacemakers to undergo MRI scans safely, addressing critical healthcare needs while ensuring effective cardiac function and imaging compatibility.</p></p>
