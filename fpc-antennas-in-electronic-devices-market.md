<p><p>FPC antennas, or Flexible Printed Circuit antennas, play a crucial role in electronic devices by providing wireless communication capabilities. The market for FPC antennas can be divided into two main types: internal and external antennas. Internal antennas are embedded within the device, offering space-saving benefits and aesthetic integration, making them popular in smartphones and wearables. In contrast, external antennas, often detachable, enhance signal strength and range, commonly used in applications requiring robust connectivity, such as routers and IoT devices.</p></p>
