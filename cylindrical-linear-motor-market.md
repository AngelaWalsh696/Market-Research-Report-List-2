<p><ul><li>Synchronous</li><li>Asynchronous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylindrical linear motor market is primarily divided into synchronous and asynchronous types. Synchronous linear motors operate with constant speed and position control, utilizing permanent magnets for high efficiency and precision. They are commonly used in applications requiring precise movement, such as robotics and automation. In contrast, asynchronous linear motors rely on electromagnetic induction, offering simpler designs and lower costs but with less precision. These motors are ideal for applications where speed is prioritized over accuracy, such as conveyor systems.</p></p>
