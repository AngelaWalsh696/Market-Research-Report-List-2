<p><ul><li>Near Infrared Illumination (NIR)</li><li>Transillumination</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vein illuminator market encompasses three primary types: Near Infrared Illumination (NIR), transillumination, and ultrasound. NIR utilizes infrared light to enhance vein visibility beneath the skin, aiding in venipuncture. Transillumination employs light to illuminate veins directly, making them more discernible for medical procedures. Ultrasound technology uses high-frequency sound waves to visualize veins, providing real-time imaging and guidance during interventions. These modalities collectively enhance the accuracy and safety of venous access in clinical settings.</p></p>
