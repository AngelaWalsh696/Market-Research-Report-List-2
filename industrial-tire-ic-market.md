<p><strong>The Industrial Tire (IC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial tire market is primarily divided into two types: bias tires and radial tires. Bias tires feature layers that crisscross at angles, providing good durability and flexibility, making them suitable for rougher terrains. Conversely, radial tires have layers arranged perpendicular to the direction of travel, offering better fuel efficiency, enhanced wear resistance, and improved performance at higher speeds. Both types serve diverse industrial applications, with the choice depending on operational needs and equipment compatibility.</p></p>
