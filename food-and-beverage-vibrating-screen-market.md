<p><p>The Food and Beverage Vibrating Screen market includes two main types: Circular Vibrating Screens and Tumbler Vibrating Screens. Circular Vibrating Screens utilize a circular motion to efficiently separate and classify materials, making them ideal for handling granules and powders in food processing. Tumbler Vibrating Screens, on the other hand, mimic hand sorting, providing gentle screening for delicate products. They are designed for higher capacity and superior efficiency, catering to various applications in the food and beverage sector.</p></p>
