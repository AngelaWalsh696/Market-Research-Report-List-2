<p><strong>The IoT RFID Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Labels</li><li>Active Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID labels market consists of two primary types: passive labels and active labels. Passive labels are cost-effective, without an internal power source, relying on energy from RFID readers to transmit data, making them ideal for tracking items like inventory and assets. Active labels, on the other hand, contain their own power source, allowing them to transmit data over longer distances and support more functionalities, making them suitable for applications such as real-time tracking of high-value items or environmental monitoring.</p></p>
