<p><p>The Anti-blue Ray Myopia Lenses market is primarily categorized into two types: spherical and aspheric lenses. Spherical lenses have a consistent curvature throughout their surface, offering a straightforward optical profile that effectively corrects myopia. In contrast, aspheric lenses feature a varying curvature, allowing for a thinner, lighter design while reducing distortion and improving peripheral vision. Both types serve the growing demand for myopia management, with aspheric lenses gaining popularity for their aesthetic and functional advantages.</p></p>
