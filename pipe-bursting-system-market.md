<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Pipe Bursting System</li><li>Cable-Pull Pipe Bursting System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pipe bursting system market features two main types: hydraulic pipe bursting systems and cable-pull pipe bursting systems. Hydraulic pipe bursting systems utilize high-pressure hydraulics to fracture existing pipes while simultaneously installing new ones, making them efficient for larger diameter pipes. In contrast, cable-pull pipe bursting systems rely on cable mechanisms to pull new pipes into place, suitable for smaller or more delicate installations. Both methods provide effective solutions for replacing aging infrastructure with minimal surface disruption.</p></p>
