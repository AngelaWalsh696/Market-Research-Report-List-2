Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) focuses on pain relief. Interferential therapy uses alternating currents for deeper tissue stimulation. Burst Mode Alternating Current provides pain relief through bursts of electrical pulses, while Micro Current Electrical Neuro Muscular Stimulators use low currents for cellular recovery.
