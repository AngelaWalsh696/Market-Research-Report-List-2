<p><p>The antiemetics market includes various drug classes for nausea and vomiting treatment. 5-HT3 receptor antagonists block serotonin receptors, while dopamine antagonists inhibit dopamine pathways. NK1 receptor antagonists target substance P. Antihistamines alleviate symptoms by blocking H1 receptors, and cannabinoids, derived from cannabis, can also reduce nausea. Benzodiazepines provide sedation and anxiety relief, anticholinergics reduce stomach motility, and steroids address inflammation that may cause vomiting. Each type plays a crucial role in managing different underlying causes of nausea and vomiting.</p></p>
