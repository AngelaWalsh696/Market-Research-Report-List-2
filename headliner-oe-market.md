<p><p>Headliner markets encompass both thermoplastic and thermoset materials used in automotive interior applications. Thermoplastics are malleable when heated and can be reshaped multiple times, making them versatile and cost-effective for production. In contrast, thermosets are solid and rigid once cured, providing enhanced durability and heat resistance, ideal for structural applications. Each type serves unique needs within the automotive sector, balancing factors like weight, cost, and performance to optimize vehicle design and functionality.</p></p>
