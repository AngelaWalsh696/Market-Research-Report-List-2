<p><p>Air core reactors are crucial components in electrical systems, primarily classified into two market types: dry type and oil immersed type. Dry type air core reactors use air as the insulation medium, making them environmentally friendly and maintenance-free, ideal for indoor applications. In contrast, oil immersed type reactors are submerged in insulating oil, offering superior cooling and handling higher power ratings, suitable for outdoor installations. Both types provide voltage regulation and improve system efficiency, catering to diverse electrical needs.</p></p>
