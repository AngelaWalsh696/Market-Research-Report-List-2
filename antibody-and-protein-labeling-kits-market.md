<p><p>The Antibody and Protein Labeling Kits market comprises various types designed for efficient visualization and detection of proteins. Fluorescent tags use fluorescent dyes to enable imaging in cell and tissue samples, providing high sensitivity. Enzymatic tags utilize enzyme-substrate reactions to produce measurable signals, often used in assays and diagnostics. Hapten tags involve small molecules that can bind to specific antibodies, facilitating detection through secondary antibodies. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the application and desired sensitivity.</p></p>
