<p><p>The bio-organic fertilizer market includes organic residue fertilizers and biofertilizers. Organic residue fertilizers are derived from plant or animal waste, enriching soil with nutrients and improving its structure. Biofertilizers, on the other hand, contain live microorganisms that promote plant growth by enhancing nutrient availability and soil health. Both types contribute to sustainable agriculture by reducing chemical fertilizer dependence, thereby improving soil ecosystems, increasing crop yields, and promoting environmental health through natural nutrient cycling.</p></p>
