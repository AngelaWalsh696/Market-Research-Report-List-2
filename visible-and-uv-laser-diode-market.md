<p><p>The visible and UV laser diode market comprises two primary types: single-mode and multi-mode lasers. Single-mode laser diodes emit a single spatial mode, providing high coherence and precision, making them ideal for applications like telecommunications and sensing. In contrast, multi-mode laser diodes can emit multiple modes simultaneously, offering higher output power and broader beam divergence, which suits applications like laser displays and industrial uses. Each type caters to specific needs based on coherence, power, and application requirements.</p></p>
