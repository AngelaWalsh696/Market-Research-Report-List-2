<p><p>Mechanical pulps are a type of pulp created through mechanical processes that grind wood chips into fibers, resulting in a product that retains a significant amount of lignin. This method contrasts with chemical pulping, which breaks down lignin and separates the cellulose fibers. Mechanical pulps are primarily used in products like newsprint, magazines, and certain types of packaging, leveraging their high yield and lower production costs.</p><p>The Mechanical Pulps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand in various applications, including printing paper and packaging materials.
