<p><p>The SiC polishing consumables market comprises two main types: SiC polishing slurries and SiC polishing pads. SiC polishing slurries are liquid mixtures containing silicon carbide particles used to achieve fine surface finishes on hard materials during semiconductor fabrication. SiC polishing pads, on the other hand, are abrasively textured materials that facilitate the mechanical polishing process, ensuring uniformity and precision in surface smoothing. Together, these consumables play a critical role in enhancing the efficiency and effectiveness of polishing operations in various applications.</p></p>
