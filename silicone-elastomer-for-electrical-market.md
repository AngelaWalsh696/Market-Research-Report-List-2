<p><p>Silicone elastomers in the electrical market are categorized into three main types: High Temperature Vulcanised (HTV), Room Temperature Vulcanised (RTV), and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR). HTV is known for its durability and resistance to extreme temperatures, making it ideal for high-performance applications. RTV cures at room temperature, offering convenience and versatility for various bonding and sealing tasks. LSR is injected for precision moldings, providing excellent flexibility and thermal stability, suitable for complex designs in electrical components.</p></p>
