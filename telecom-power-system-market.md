<p><p>The telecom power system market is categorized into two main types: on-grid and off-grid systems. On-grid systems are connected to the main electricity grid, allowing telecom operators to access a reliable power supply while minimizing costs. In contrast, off-grid systems operate independently, utilizing renewable energy sources like solar or wind power, ideal for remote locations without grid access. Both systems are essential for ensuring uninterrupted telecom services, with each type catering to specific operational needs and environmental conditions.</p></p>
