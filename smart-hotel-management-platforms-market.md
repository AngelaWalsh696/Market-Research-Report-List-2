<p><p>Smart hotel management platforms are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based systems operate over the internet, allowing hotels to access management tools and data from anywhere, enhancing flexibility and reducing infrastructure costs. In contrast, on-premise solutions are installed directly on the hotel's local servers, offering more control over data security and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. Both types cater to different operational needs and preferences within the hospitality industry.</p></p>
