<p><p>The genetic testing services market encompasses various types, each serving distinct purposes. Prenatal testing assesses genetic conditions in fetuses, while newborn screening identifies disorders immediately after birth for early intervention. Predictive and presymptomatic testing evaluates the risk of developing genetic conditions in asymptomatic individuals. Pharmacogenomic testing analyzes how genes affect a person's response to medications, allowing for tailored treatments. Together, these services enhance healthcare by providing critical information for diagnosis, prevention, and personalized medicine strategies.</p></p>
