<p><p>The temporary pacemaker market comprises three main types: single-chamber, dual-chamber, and triple-chamber temporary pacemakers. Single-chamber temporary pacemakers stimulate either the atrium or ventricle, suitable for less complex cases. Dual-chamber pacemakers engage both chambers, providing coordinated cardiac function and better management of specific arrhythmias. Triple-chamber temporary pacemakers involve three leads, offering advanced support for heart failure patients by facilitating synchronized contractions of all chambers, enhancing overall cardiac output and therapeutic efficacy. Each type serves distinct clinical needs.</p></p>
