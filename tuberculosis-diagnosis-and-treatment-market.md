<p><p>The tuberculosis diagnosis and treatment market comprises various methods. Laboratory testing involves analyzing biological samples to detect Mycobacterium tuberculosis. Nucleic acid tests detect the genetic material of the bacteria, providing rapid results. The Mantoux test assesses skin reaction to tuberculin for infection indication, while radiography uses X-rays to visualize lung infection or disease extent. Drug susceptibility tests determine the effectiveness of antibiotics against the bacteria, guiding treatment decisions and ensuring appropriate management of tuberculosis cases.</p></p>
