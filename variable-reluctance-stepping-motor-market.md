<p><p>A Variable Reluctance Stepping Motor (VRSM) is a type of stepper motor that operates on the principle of varying reluctance in its magnetic circuit. It achieves precise control of rotational position and speed by utilizing a series of magnetic poles and winding configurations. Unlike other stepper motors, VRSMs have no permanent magnets, making them lighter and often more efficient. Due to their inherent characteristics, these motors are widely used in applications requiring high precision, such as robotics, CNC machinery, and medical devices.</p><p>The Variable Reluctance Stepping Motor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period.
