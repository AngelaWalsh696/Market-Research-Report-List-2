<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Airbag Inflator market features three main types: pyrotechnic inflators, stored gas inflators, and hybrid inflators. Pyrotechnic inflators use a chemical reaction to generate gas quickly upon impact, making them efficient for rapid deployment. Stored gas inflators utilize pressurized gas for inflation, providing a consistent and controlled deployment. Hybrid inflators combine both technologies, offering advantages of quick deployment and reliable performance. Each type caters to specific safety requirements and design considerations in commercial vehicles.</p></p>
