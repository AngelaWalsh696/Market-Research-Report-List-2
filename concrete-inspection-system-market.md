<p><p>The Concrete Inspection System market encompasses various non-destructive testing techniques, notably Ultrasonic Inspection and CT (Computed Tomography) Inspection. Ultrasonic Inspection employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws, ensuring the integrity of concrete structures. It is effective for assessing voids and cracks. In contrast, CT Inspection offers a detailed 3D imaging approach, allowing for comprehensive analysis of concrete's internal characteristics, including density variations and hidden defects. Both methods enhance the evaluation and maintenance of concrete infrastructure.</p></p>
