<p><ul><li>Uniaxial Crystal</li><li>Biaxial Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The birefringent materials market is categorized into two main types: uniaxial and biaxial crystals. Uniaxial crystals have a single optical axis, exhibiting two distinct refractive indices, making them suitable for applications in optics and photonics. Biaxial crystals, on the other hand, possess two optical axes and exhibit three different refractive indices, allowing for more complex manipulation of light. The choice between these materials depends on specific application requirements in industries such as telecommunications, imaging, and laser technology.</p></p>
