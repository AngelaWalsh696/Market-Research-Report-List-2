<p><ul><li>Real-time Location Systems (RTLS)</li><li>Radio-frequency Identification (RFID)</li><li>Ultrasound</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hospital asset management encompasses various market types that enhance the tracking and management of medical equipment and personnel. Real-time Location Systems (RTLS) utilize wireless technology to pinpoint asset locations in real-time. Radio-frequency Identification (RFID) employs tags and readers for efficient inventory management and tracking. Ultrasound technology uses sound waves for precise location tracking of assets. Infrared systems utilize light signals to monitor asset movement and status, promoting operational efficiency and improving patient care in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
