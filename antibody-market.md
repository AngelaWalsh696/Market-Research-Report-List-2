<p><p>The antibody market is segmented into various types based on their function and structure, primarily including IgM, IgG, IgA, and other antibodies. IgM is the first antibody produced in response to infections, offering initial defense. IgG is the most abundant and plays a crucial role in long-term immunity and pathogen neutralization. IgA is primarily found in mucosal areas, protecting body surfaces. Other antibodies encompass less common types and specialized functions, contributing to the diverse landscape of immune response and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
